Biography

I am a fifth year Ph.D. student with the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago, where I’m advised by Jason Polakis. My research spans in the areas of Web Security and Privacy, with focus on device and browser fingerprinting and user tracking. I received my Bsc (2016) and Msc (2019) degrees from the Computer Science Department of University of Crete. During my undergrad and masters I was also a member of the DiSCS lab at Forth where I worked under the supervision of Sotiris Ioannidis.
